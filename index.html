About

Hunter August Shriver is 21 Years old from St. Paul Minnesota. Hunter's passions are coding, music and good coffee. Working with music taught attention to detail, and breaking things down into smaller components to understand and learn them.
First seeking a career in music production, it was nessecary to develop strong self-teaching skills, and how to tap into the ever growing resource of knowledge that is the internet today. Learning how to work with a 'DAW' (Digital Audio Workstation) proved later to be quite similar to working within a code text editor and in some ways creating a web application is similar to creating a song digitally.

Working in food service taught great patience, with self, and others. In addition, the attention to detail in creation of a quality product. These previous expiriences helped develop Hunter's aptitude for code, in particular web development. Hunter recently graduated from the UofM's Coding Bootcamp, gaining a certification as a Full stack Web developer. Main technical skills include: Html5, Css3, Javascript, Jquery Node.js and React.js
