About

Hunter August Shriver is 21 years old from St. Paul, Minnesota. Hunter's passions are coding, music and good coffee. Hunter finds similarities in coding and making music. When writing music or code you need to follow logical rules to ensure the 'moving parts' will work together correctly. First seeking a career in music production, Hunter developed strong self-teaching skills and perseverance. Learning how to work with a Digital Audio Workstation (DAW) proved later to be quite similar to working within a code text editor. Hunter recently graduated from the UofMN's Coding Bootcamp, gaining a certification as a Full Stack Web developer. Main technical skills include: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Jquery Node.js and React.js.
